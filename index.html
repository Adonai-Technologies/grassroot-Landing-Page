THE HEAVENLY SARAPHINE

The Heavenly Saraphine is a non-denominational evangelistic group that spreads the gospel through contemporary music. The group is based in Tema , Ghana and over the years has been the pioneer of contemporary gospel music in Tema House of Power Ministries International.
